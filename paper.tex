\note{ISIN-499-Capstone \\ Spring 2011 \\ April 24, 2011}
\abstract{

Social media has exploded onto the Internet and with it comes a host of privacy,
technological, and legal challenges. Privacy concerns are numerous but the
solutions to those concerns are surfacing everyday. Exploring the privacy
policies, terms of use statements, and issues related to social media, this paper
gives a definition of social media and it's implications. This paper also explores the
issues related to the geo-tagging of photographs and describes some tools and
techniques that can help a user keep their personal information private on the Internet.

}
